Summerville Art Club

Where Passion

Meets Purpose

Summerville Art Club was established in 2015 by a dedicated group of artists, educators, and community leaders in response to the need for hands-on art education in Summerville. Located in the heart of Downtown Summerville, it's the area's only nonprofit community art center providing hands-on arts instruction and engagement. Our vision is to be a recognized hub for accessible arts education and experiences through our work to to energize and empower the arts community.
